Scarlet Drone

Develop three variations of a futuristic vehicle with a real-world narrative and purpose.

The Scarlet Drone is an emergency response drone used by emergency services, such as police officers, firefighters, or volunteer rescue teams. Instead of sending in first responders to dangerous terrain and unnecessarily putting them at risk, these drones can be remotely controlled to gain a quick birds-eye view of any situation.

Aerial footage from the site of an emergency can be viewed in real-time to provide rescuers with information about the location of an injured person or about the scale and impact of a disaster. This allows emergency responders to coordinate relief efforts, saving time and money.
