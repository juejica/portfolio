Jojo Rabbit

Redesign and animate the title sequence of an already existing film.

This title sequence aimed to function as a hybrid design work that represented and embodied the original film.

Jojo Rabbit is a comedy-drama war film about Johannes “Jojo” Betzler, a ten-year-old Hitler Youth member who finds out that his mother is hiding a Jewish girl in their attic. His nickname became “Jojo Rabbit” after failing to strangle a bunny when ordered to do so at a Nazi youth training camp.

As the movie was portrayed from the perspective of a 10-year-old, I aimed to capture the innocent and whimsical nature of Jojo’s imagination.

Frames were drawn frame-by-frame in Procreate and brought into After Effects for compositing and transitions.
